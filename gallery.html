A visual journey through the life, temples, and legacy of Sree Narayana Guru

This gallery presents a visual tribute to Sree Narayana Guru — his sacred images, the temples he consecrated, the historic events of his life, and the continuing legacy of his social and spiritual transformation of Kerala and humanity.

Images shown are representative illustrations. To contribute photos of Guru temples, festivals, and related sites, please contact us at sivagiriashram.com
