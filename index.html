Editorial Note

One light, many tongues; one truth, many songs.

Every text on this website is presented directly on the site. There are no YouTube links, no outbound reading journeys, and no embedded external text libraries.

The collection below is written as a devotional and educational digital companion in the Guru's philosophical orbit, with original translations, meditations, and commentarial renderings.

Philosophy

Advaita without abstraction

The library returns again and again to non-duality as lived clarity: the self, the world, and the divine are not enemies to each other but doors to one presence.

Social Reform

Equality as sacred practice

Anti-caste thought appears here not as a side theme, but as a direct consequence of spiritual insight: if truth is one, human dignity cannot be divided.

Devotion

Prayer with tenderness

Hymns, vows, invocations, and contemplative songs are shaped for chanting, inward repetition, and family reading across generations.
