About Sivagiri

Sivagiri Mutt, Varkala

Sivagiri Mutt in Varkala stands as one of the most important spiritual and social reform centers inspired by Sree Narayana Guru. It continues the mission of inner uplift, social equality, education, and service.

The ashram atmosphere here is calm and contemplative, while the mission is active and outward-looking: making spirituality meaningful through learning, discipline, compassion, and community transformation.
