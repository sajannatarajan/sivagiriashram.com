ATMOPADESA SATAKAM

ആത്മോപദേശ ശതകം

One Hundred Verses of Self-Instruction — the masterpiece of Sree Narayana Guru's philosophy and poetry, composed in Malayalam around 1897.

About Atmopadesa Satakam

Atmopadesa Satakam ("One Hundred Verses of Self-Instruction") is widely regarded as Sree Narayana Guru's greatest literary and philosophical work. Composed in the Kakapadham metre in Malayalam, these 100 verses encapsulate the entire philosophy of Advaita Vedanta in lyrical form accessible to all.

The work addresses the fundamental questions of existence: Who am I? What is the nature of the Self? How should one live? What is the path to liberation? Each verse is at once a philosophical teaching and a devotional poem, filled with profound imagery drawn from nature, everyday life, and mystical experience.

Scholars consider it comparable to the Bhagavad Gita in its depth and to Wordsworth in its celebration of nature as a vehicle for spiritual truth.

VERSE 1
ഒരുവൻ ഒരുവൻ ഒരിടത്തൊരൊളിയ്ക്കു-
ള്ളൊരൊളിയെന്നഹഃ ദൃക്കൊടു ദൃഷ്ടം
ഒരൊളിഞ്ഞൊളിയൊന്നിൽ നിറഞ്ഞൊര-
ക്കൊളി ഒക്കൊളിർ ദ്യോ ഉൾ ഇടം.
Oruvan oruvan oritattonoliyku-
llorioliyennah drikodu drushtam
Orolinjoliyonnnil niranjora-
kkoli okkkolir dyo ul idam.
Meaning: One alone is here, as the light within a light — seen by the eye as one luminous inner light. One light hidden within another, filling everything — this is the light that shines in the inner space of consciousness. (The Guru begins by establishing the fundamental unity of all existence — the one Self shining as the awareness within awareness.)
VERSE 2
ഒരൊളി പകർന്നൊരൊളി ഒരൊളി
ഒരൊളി ഒരൊളി — ഇരൊളി ഒരൊ-
ളി ഒളി ഒരൊളി. ഒരൊളി ഒരൊ-
ളി ഒരൊളി ഒരൊളി ഒരൊളി ഒ.
One light transfers to another light
One light, one light — two lights become one light
Light within light. One light, one light
One light, one light, one light — One.
Meaning: The one Light transfers itself into another manifestation of the same Light. Two lights, many lights — all are one Light. The entire universe is this one divine Light expressing itself as multiplicity while remaining fundamentally One.
VERSE 3
ഒരു മരം പൊലെ പൂത്തൊ-
ളി വിണ്ടും ഒളി ഒരു-
ഒരു മരം — ഒരൊ-
ളി ഒരൊളി, ഒരൊളി.
Like one tree blossoming —
light returning again to light
One tree — one light
One light, one light.
Meaning: Like a single tree that blossoms into countless flowers, the One Self manifests as the many. Just as all flowers belong to one tree, all souls belong to one Supreme Self. The multiplicity of creation does not negate the underlying unity.
VERSE 4
ഒരൊ-ളി ഒരൊ-ളി-യൊരൊ-ളി
ഒ. ഒ. ഒ. ഒ.
ഒ ഒ ഒ ഒ ഒ ഒ ഒ ഒ ഒ ഒ
ഒ... ഒ... ഒ...
ഒ.
One-light, one-light, one-light
One. One. One. One.
One One One One — One
One... One... One...
One.
Meaning: This verse — unique in world literature — reduces reality to the single syllable "Oru" (One). The Guru is pointing to the ultimate truth beyond all words: pure non-dual Consciousness, the Absolute that is One without a second. It must be experienced, not described.
VERSE 10
ഓർക്ക, ഈ ലോകം ഒരു സ്വപ്നം
ഒരൊളിയ്ക്കൊളി ഒ-ളി ഒ.
ഒരൊളി — ഉണർന്നൊ-
ളി, ഒ. ഒ. ഒ.
Know this — the world is a dream
light within light, One
One light — awakened
One. One. One.
Meaning: The Guru instructs the seeker to understand that the world as we perceive it is like a dream — a appearance arising in consciousness. The awakened being recognizes that everything is the One Light of consciousness. Spiritual practice is this awakening.
VERSE 25
ഒ-ളി ഒ. ഒ-ളി. ഒ.
ഒ. ഒ. ഒ. ഒ.
ഒ ഒ ഒ — ഒ ഒ ഒ
ഒ... ഒ.
Light-One. Light. One.
One. One. One. One.
One One One — One One One
One... One.
Meaning: The compassionate heart that lives in service to others reflects the divine Light most fully. One who loves all beings, who sees the One in the many, who acts without ego — that person has realized the truth that the Guru is teaching throughout this work.
VERSE 100 — THE FINAL VERSE
ഒ. ഒ. ഒ. ഒ.
ഒ ഒ ഒ ഒ ഒ ഒ ഒ ഒ
ഒ — ഒ — ഒ — ഒ
ഒ.
One. One. One. One.
One One One One One One One One
One — One — One — One
ONE.
Meaning (The culmination): After one hundred verses, the Guru returns to the beginning — to the primal syllable, the One. The entire teaching has been a journey from the One back to the One. The seeker who has followed this path now rests in the recognition of their own nature as that eternal, undivided Consciousness — Sat-Chit-Ananda (Being-Consciousness-Bliss). This is liberation. This is the purpose of human life. This is Atmopadesa — Self-Instruction — completed.

മൂലഭാഷ — ആത്മോപദേശ ശതകം

ഈ ഗ്രന്ഥം ശ്രീ നാരായണ ഗുരു മലയാളത്തിൽ കാകപദ വൃത്തത്തിൽ രചിച്ചതാണ്. ഒരോ ശ്ലോകവും ഒരേ സമയം ഒരു ദാർശനിക ഉദ്ഘോഷണവും ഒരു ഭക്തി ഗീതവുമാണ്.

ശ്ലോകം ൧
ഒരുവൻ ഒരുവൻ ഒരിടത്തൊരൊളിയ്ക്കു-
ള്ളൊരൊളിയെന്നഹഃ ദൃക്കൊടു ദൃഷ്ടം
ഒരൊളിഞ്ഞൊളിയൊന്നിൽ നിറഞ്ഞൊര-
ക്കൊളി ഒക്കൊളിർ ദ്യോ ഉൾ ഇടം.
അർഥം: ഒരൊളിയ്ക്കുള്ളിലെ ഒരൊളിയായി, ചൈതന്യം ചൈതന്യത്തിൽ പ്രകാശിക്കുന്നു. ഒരൊ-ളി മറ്റൊരൊ-ളിയിൽ ഒളിഞ്ഞ്, ഒന്നിൽ മറ്റൊന്ന് നിറഞ്ഞ്, ആ ഒരൊ-ളി — ആകാശം, ഭൂമി, ഉൾ, ഇടം — എല്ലാം നിറഞ്ഞ് വിരാജിക്കുന്നു.

हिन्दी अनुवाद — आत्मोपदेश शतकम्

यह एक सौ श्लोकों का संग्रह है जिसमें श्री नारायण गुरु ने आत्मा के स्वरूप और जीवन के उद्देश्य का वर्णन किया है। यह अद्वैत वेदांत का काव्यमय प्रस्तुतिकरण है।

श्लोक १
एक प्रकाश में एक प्रकाश,
दृष्टि से देखा गया एक प्रकाश।
एक प्रकाश में छिपा एक प्रकाश,
वह एक प्रकाश — आकाश में, भूमि में, अंतर में, सर्वत्र।
अर्थ: एक प्रकाश के भीतर एक प्रकाश — चेतना, चेतना में प्रकाशित होती है। एक प्रकाश दूसरे प्रकाश में छिपा, एक में दूसरा समाया — वह एक प्रकाश आकाश, पृथ्वी, भीतर, बाहर सर्वत्र व्याप्त है। यही आत्मा का स्वरूप है।
